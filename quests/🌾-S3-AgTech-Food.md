## 📖 Theory Bite

Agricultural technology and food security combine **biology, logistics, data science, and policy**.

| 🧩 Concept | 💡 What it means |
|---------|---------------|
| 🔄 Food system stages | Production → Processing → Distribution → Retail → Consumption → Waste |
| 🍽️ Food security (FAO) | Availability, Access, Utilization, Stability — all 4 must hold |
| 🛰️ Precision agriculture | Sensors, drones, data → optimize water, fertilizer, pesticides |
| 📦 Post-harvest loss | 30-40% of food never eaten — biggest leverage is distribution, not production |

> 💼 **Daily reality:** Field work, supply chain analysis, data collection, and bridging the gap between farmers and technologists.

### 🗝️ Quest S3.2 — Starter Project Brief
**⏱ Time:** ~45 min | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ Intermediate | **Unlock:** S3.3

**Quest:** Design a one-page brief for an AgTech starter project.

**Steps:**
1. 🎯 Pick one agricultural problem you care about
2. 📋 Write the brief:

```
🌱 STARTER PROJECT BRIEF
├── 🎯 Problem Statement (2 sentences)
├── 🔧 Technology Approach (what tech would you use?)
├── 🛠 Required Skills (list 3-5)
├── 📅 30-Day Plan
│   ├── 📆 Week 1: Research & understand the problem
│   ├── 📆 Week 2: Design the solution
│   ├── 📆 Week 3: Build the smallest possible prototype
│   └── 📆 Week 4: Test & document
└── 📏 Success Metric (one measurable outcome)
```

> 🪞 **Reflection:** Could you see yourself working on problems where results are measured in harvests, not release cycles?
